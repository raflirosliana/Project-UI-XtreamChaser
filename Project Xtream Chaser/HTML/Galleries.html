Story Mode Will Bring You As A Daimyo Servant In Battle

As a samurai, our main character is ordered to protect our land from the attacker and attack to take over another land. Story mode bring you to an unlimited journey of a samurai in japan around 1100 AD. The main quest can be obtain from our Daimyo, and there are also a hundreds side quest during this mode. you can found a great weapon and armor that can be upgrade in the local merchant.

Online PvP Againts Another Player Online

Online Player Vs Player will bring you to a fierce battle between Samurai. You will take into a one on one combat with another player. You can sieze the loot of your opponent if you win. from this mode you can obtain a unique item that u can used in 3 game mode. Or you can also buy it from the online market. Online Role Play Gameplay Overview Gallery

Online Role Play In Domestic Server

Online Role play is the most exciting part in this game. You will meet another player around the globe in online server. You can make your own clan, grow your clan, and expand your clan territory. You have to choose your role in this mode, whether it is a Clan Leader, Guards, Blacksmith, Archer, or Warrior. Each role have a task that must be done. You can use the items from the PvP mode in this game. Build, Grow, and Expand your clan.
